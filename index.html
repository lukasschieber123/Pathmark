How it works
The Globe

Double-click anywhere on the globe to drop a pin at that location. Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom. You need an active trip before you can add pins.

Trips

Use the top-left menu to create a trip. You can have multiple trips — switch between them anytime. Each trip has its own set of pins connected by flight-path arcs. Rename or delete a trip from the same menu.

Pins

Click a pin to open its detail panel on the right. You can give it a name, add travel dates, flight info, hotel details, and notes. Pins are numbered in the order they were added and connected by arcs in that order.

Search

Type any city, landmark, or country in the search bar at the top. Click a result to fly the globe there. Results are ranked by popularity.

Compass

The compass in the top-right corner rotates as you spin the globe. Click it to snap north back to the top.
