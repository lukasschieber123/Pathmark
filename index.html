Privacy Policy

Pathmark does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All trip data you create is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and never leaves your device.

The app loads map tiles from OpenFreeMap and uses Photon (powered by OpenStreetMap) for place search. These services may log standard request data such as your IP address, subject to their own privacy policies.
